About Filomena

Filomena holds an MSc, a PhD, and is a certified Pilates teacher specialising in Traditional Pilates, Mat Pilates, Functional Reformer, and Wunda Chair. She is fully accredited by the YMCA Awards and CIMSPA.

She has undertaken continued professional development in Neurology applied to Pilates, Knee Arthroplasty Prehabilitation and Surgical Rehabilitation, Hip and Knee Strengthening, and Breathwork.

Her commitment to Pilates and holistic fitness began in 2023 after overcoming full-body hypotonia and muscle atrophy through intensive physical therapy and a rehabilitation-focused Pilates programme she designed herself. Her extensive study of biomechanics, anatomy, holistic rehabilitation, and mental rehearsal techniques ultimately led her to pursue formal Pilates training.

Today, Filomena’s practice focuses on helping clients reconnect with their bodies, improve mobility, develop postural strength, and build long-term physical resilience. Her approach combines traditional and contemporary Pilates with functional movement, endurance training, and evidence-based techniques such as breathwork and active visualisation to support lasting transformation, nervous system regulation, and confident movement.

The Mantra:
The way we train our bodies shapes the way we train our minds.
