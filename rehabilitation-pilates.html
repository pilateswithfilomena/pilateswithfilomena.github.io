Injury Rehabilitation & Post-Rehabilitation Pilates

Recover stronger and move with confidence through our personalized Pilates-based injury rehabilitation programs. Whether you are healing from an injury or dealing with chronic pain, our approach blends functional movement, posture and movement correction, and modified contemporary and classical Pilates exercises to build overall body stability, endurance and strength to support a safe and sustainable recovery or/and post-recovery.

Our Pilates programs are designed to:

  • Rebuild strength and stability in weakened areas
  • Improve joint mobility and muscle control
  • Reduce pain and prevent future injuries
  • Restore healthy movement patterns
  • Support long-term physical independence

Every session is designed around your unique condition and goals. Using chair, standing, mat, and reformer Pilates, exercises are adapted to your ability level to ensure safe, effective progress. You can choose from private Pilates rehabilitation sessions in person (London, UK) or online, making it flexible and accessible wherever you are.

We can work closely with your healthcare/therapist provider when needed to ensure that your Pilates program complements your medical recovery plan.

Ideal for:

  • Back, neck and shoulder tension/pain
  • Joint instability, injury, osteoarthritis and replacement
  • Muscle imbalances
  • Physiological Muscle atrophy - hypotonia and hypotrophy

Why Choose Pilates with Filomena for Rehabilitation?

You might be wondering, "Why should I trust you?" I've been where you are. I created this program after healing from physiological muscle atrophy and chronic back pain both related to my work and a sedentary lifestyle. Pilates changed my life, and now I help others do the same. Read my full story here.

Pilates is a flexible method that offers a low-impact, evidence-based way to improve functional strength, posture, and coordination after injury. With a focus on body awareness and controlled movement powerful tool for recovery and injury prevention.
