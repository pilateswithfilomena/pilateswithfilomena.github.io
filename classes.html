Pilates Classes

Gym Group Classes

These classes offer a full-body workout combining classical and contemporary Pilates with functional exercises. The focus is on developing core strength, building overall muscle strength, and improving posture.

Please refer to our Wembley Fitness classes, Pilates (Contrology) at the gym's studio, schedule:

  • Thursdays, from 17:00 to 18:00
  • Fridays, from 19:00 to 20:00
  • Saturdays, from 11:55 to 12:55

Please arrive 5 minutes earlier so that we can start on time.

Make sure to wear comfortable clothes such, as a t-shirt, sports leggings, and Pilates socks.

All equipment will be provided by the gym. However, feel free to bring your own mat, if you prefer.

If you are new, please fill in your PAR-Q before class.
