About Filomena

My name is Filomena, I am a certified Pilates Instructor. I am a former architect and PhD in the fields of Project Development and Sustainability.

My passion for Pilates and holistic wellness began over two years ago. By the end of 2022, I started to suffer from acute back pain, which rapidly spread to my neck, shoulders, and torso. This was followed by intense muscle spasms, a gradual loss of strength, and severe stiffness. In early 2023, I was diagnosed with head-to-toe hypotonia and hypotrophy, radiating into my limbs. My body felt like a prison.

I started an intensive physical therapy treatment, which later included Pilates. This was a game-changer! Month after month, I regained a little more independence. I was able to make my own bed without spasms, drive again, vacuum, and eventually go for long walks and to the supermarket.

Pilates helped me regain strength and rebalance my muscles. At the clinic, I trained with apparatus (reformer, Cadillac, chair, and barrel), which I didn't have access to at home. It was also the first time I designed my own rehabilitation mat-based Pilates routine, which I unknowingly followed consistently every day for nearly two years to complement and accelerate my recovery.

With the help of doctors and physiotherapists, I educated myself about muscle conditions, causes, treatments, biomechanics, and anatomy. Later, I decided to pursue formal education in the Classical Pilates method to help others prevent and recover from these and other conditions that can drastically impact lives.

My formal education through courses and Continuing Professional Development is ongoing, as is my passion for sharing my story and raising awareness about muscle loss and its devastating consequences among women, the most affected group, especially as we age. Currently, I still follow a Pilates routine that I complement with hypertrophy training to minimize muscle loss and improve muscle development and quality.
